About Us

Welcome to Karpos Zois, where tradition meets taste. Born out of a love for authentic Cypriot delights, we take pride in offering a curated selection of Mediterranean-inspired treats. From the finest nuts and dried fruits to handcrafted nut butters, teas, and wines, our collection is designed to delight your senses.

Whether you're searching for the perfect gift or simply treating yourself, each visit promises exceptional quality, unforgettable flavors, and a connection to the heart of the Mediterranean. Let us bring a piece of Cyprus to your table.
